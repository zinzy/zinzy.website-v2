Heya, I'm Zinzy, and I’ve been yelling at Internet clouds since 1997.

You've reached my home on the Internet. In my 90s Spice Girls-themed bedroom, I taught myself how to build websites. I haven’t been the same since. At some point during this journey, making things for the Internet became my profession.

By now, I have sixteen years of professional experience researching, designing, and building digital products. I'm currently wrapping up my time at Leeruniek, an edtech company.

Before this, I spent five years leading design at travel technology market leader Airtrade. Before that, I founded Studio Zinzy in 2007, a design boutique working across publishing, healthcare, fine arts, travel, and entertainment.

As a designer, I'm specialized in making things for people at work. I'm currently not available for contracting, side projects, or fulltime roles.
