My work

For the past 16 years, I've gained experience researching, designing, and building digital products and services. At present, I manage UX at Leeruniek, where we take a data-driven approach to equitable education. Having sharpened my pencils in both consumer and enterprise spaces, I now specialize in enterprise SaaS products and their services.
